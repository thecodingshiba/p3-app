Welcome to a free interactive dashboard ECO-POP to explore and analyse Census and GDP data

1) Polulation & GDP analysis by country

The following tool is designed to study the numerous population characteristics and the GDP statistics for a individual countries for a selected time frame. The two windows feature allow us to do comparative analysis for two countries simultaneously .

2) Global population anaylsis

The following tool is designed to study the numerous population characteristics and the GDP statistics for all countries and/or subregions together.

3) Geographical visualisation

The following tool's geographic visualisation facilitates a comparative analysis of characteristics of GDP and population statistics for every nation on the entire globe.
